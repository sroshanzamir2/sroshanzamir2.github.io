About Me

I am a third-year math Ph.D. student at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln working under the supervision of Dr. Alexandra Seceleanu. My research interests are commutative algebra and geometric invariant theory. I am currently reading Lectures in Invariant theory by Igor Dolgachev. I completed my Master's at the University of Minnesota Duluth under the supervision of Dr. Joe Gallian. I am also interested in cross-sections of humanities and mathematics (see my Research page and this talk).) In my free time I enjoy weightlifting.
