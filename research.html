Research

Research Interests I am interested in commutative algebra and algebraic geometry and their interplay with combinatorics, representation theory, geometric group theory and invariant theory.
  1. Interpolation problems: double points in weighted projective spaces and points with higher multiplicities in the projective plane.
  2. Ideal containment problem for ordinary and symbolic powers and related conjectures such as the stable Harbourne conjecture.
  3. Lefschetz properties of certain algebras associated to geometric constructions such the Böröcky configuration.
